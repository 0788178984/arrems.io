Frequently Asked Questions

What is ARREMS?

ARREMS is a platform that allows users to explore properties using augmented reality and virtual tours.

How do I create an account?

You can create an account by clicking on the "Register" button on the homepage and filling out the required information.

How can I reset my password?

If you forgot your password, click on "Forgot password?" on the sign-in page and follow the instructions to reset it.

Can I view properties without an account?

Yes, you can browse properties without an account, but you will need to register to save favorites or schedule tours.

Who can I contact for support?

You can reach our support team via the contact form on our website or by emailing support@arrem.com.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is ARREMS?

ARREMS is a platform that allows users to explore properties using augmented reality and virtual tours.

How do I create an account?

You can create an account by clicking on the "Register" button on the homepage and filling out the required information.

Can I view properties without an account?

Yes, you can browse properties without an account, but you will need to register to save your favorites and access additional features.

Who can I contact for support?

You can contact our support team via the "Contact" section on our website or email us at support@arrem.com.
